About Me

I'm currently living in New York City and have recently worked at Quikirr, a bootstrapped startup building an Excel productivity suite for Private Equity and financial institutions.

Before Quikirr, I worked at Jackpine Technologies. I've also built tools for Hollywood producers to work on audio-visual experiences, including a new fluid simulation that reacts to audio input.

I'm passionate about robotics and am aiming to secure a job in this area or something related to hardware.

I believe that life is an adventure where one must confront chaos to find meaning.

I enjoy lifting weights. I was inspired by John Meadows during my senior year of high school.

Interests

AI and robots will eventually replace humans in boring and dangerous tasks. This is something we should welcome with open arms and help accelerate as much as possible.

I’m passionate about integrating AI into our daily lives. My main interests are in collaborative decision-making between autonomous systems and humans. I enjoy building systems the general population can interact with directly. Naturally, the main fields I’m most interested in are AI, robotics, and human-robot interaction.

My other technical interests are in space and defense. I have a gut feeling I'll end up working on space robots once space is commercialized more.

I love keeping up with the startup world and am interested in angel investing.

Recently, I've been inspired by the work on physical simulations and fluid dynamics, particularly after exploring Karl Sims' projects. I'm working on a new fluid simulation that takes in audio and reacts accordingly.
