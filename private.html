May 15, 2024

Morning Reflections

The quiet hours of early morning have always been my sanctuary. Before the world awakens, there's a stillness that allows thoughts to flow without interruption.

The sun is new each day.

— Heraclitus

I've been contemplating how each sunrise offers the possibility of renewal. No matter what happened yesterday, today brings fresh opportunities to create, to connect, to be present.

May 10, 2024

On Creative Persistence

Started a new project today after weeks of creative block. There's something powerful about pushing through resistance.

Don't think about making art, just get it done. Let everyone else decide if it's good or bad, whether they love it or hate it. While they are deciding, make even more art.

— Andy Warhol

I'm learning that inspiration often comes after you've already begun. The act of creating itself generates the energy to continue creating.

May 5, 2024

Sunset Thoughts

Watched the sunset from my window today. The sky was painted in shades of blood orange and deep purple.

There's a sunrise and a sunset every single day, and they're absolutely free. Don't miss so many of them.

— Jo Walton

Nature provides daily spectacles if we only take the time to notice them. These moments of beauty require no tickets, no reservations—just our attention.
